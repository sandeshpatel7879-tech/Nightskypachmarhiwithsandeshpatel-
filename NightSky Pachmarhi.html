About Your Guide

Sandesh Patel

Astronomy Guide & Stargazing Expert

Welcome to Stargazing Pachmarhi! I'm Sandesh Patel, your personal guide to the wonders of the night sky. With years of experience in astronomy and a deep passion for celestial wonders, I've made it my mission to share the beauty of the cosmos with visitors to Pachmarhi.

My journey began as a young boy fascinated by the stars, and over the years, I've honed my knowledge through extensive observation and study. Now, I offer guided stargazing sessions that combine scientific knowledge with the sheer wonder of looking up at a sky filled with stars, planets, and distant galaxies.

Pachmarhi's unique location offers some of the clearest night skies in central India, making it the perfect place for stargazing. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced astronomy enthusiast, I'll ensure you have an unforgettable experience under the stars.
